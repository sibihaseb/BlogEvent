About Shekinah Haitian SDA Church

Shekinah Haitian Seventh-day Adventist Church was established in 2005 with a mission to serve the Haitian community and beyond. Our name "Shekinah" refers to the dwelling or settling of the divine presence of God, which is at the heart of everything we do.

We are a vibrant, multicultural congregation committed to sharing the love of Jesus Christ through worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and evangelism. Our services are conducted in both Haitian Creole and English to serve our diverse community.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who love God, serve others, and transform communities.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope, healing, and spiritual growth, reflecting God's love in our community and beyond.
